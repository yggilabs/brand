How does buying another product help me to "buy less"?

We think that when you brand your clothing, you become more invested in it. Not only has the monetary investment increased but, the amount of time and thought as well. It's our hope that this investment changes and reinforces your behavior to align with the buy less principle.

How much of my purchase goes to charity?

When a badge is activated $2 is donated to the selected charity.
