How does it work?

When you re:brand a product you simply attach a badge to your product. The badge is both a commitment to the principles above as well as, a tangible reminder of that commitment for the future. We currently offer both iron-on and hand sew options for our badges.

Each badge comes with an activation code and a unique website address. When you visit the website address and activate your badge using the code, you can select which charity you would like that badge's purchase to support.

Since each purchase comes with a pair of badges, we encourage our customers to give one of the badges away after using one. Each badge has a seperate activation and choice of charity.
